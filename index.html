About Us

Start Walking Foundation is dedicated to empowering communities across Africa, starting with Kenya. Our mission is to promote education, sustainability, and community development, ensuring a brighter future for those we serve.

  • Supply Essential Educational Resources

    We work to Provide learning materials and scholarships to talented yet vulnerable students to support their academic journey.

  • Establish Food Banks

    We work to ensure students have access to nutritious meals to enhance their physical and mental well-being, enabling them to focus on their education.

  • Strengthening Communities

    We aim to implement programs addressing triple threats (poverty, gender inequality, and lack of education), early marriages, sensitize young people on drugs and substance abuse, and HIV/AIDs as well as taking care of the infected and affected persons. We also aim to eradicate period poverty by providing menstrual hygiene products to girls in rural areas, reducing early marriages and empowering them to stay in school.

Programs and Initiatives

Our programs and initiatives are designed to create meaningful impact by addressing critical needs, empowering communities, and fostering sustainable development. From education and healthcare to environmental sustainability and economic growth, each initiative reflects our commitment to making a difference and building a better tomorrow.

Educational Support

Provide 200 scholarships annually to talented students facing financial challenges.

Nutritious Meal Programs

Establish food banks to serve nutritious meals to 1,000 students monthly. Partner with local organizations to ensure sustainable food supply.

Pad Banks Initiative

Provide menstrual hygiene products to 1,000 girls annually to combat period poverty. Conduct awareness sessions to reduce stigma around menstruation and promote health education.

Community Empowerment Programs

Organize workshops on leadership, gender equality, and life skills. Develop campaigns addressing early marriages and empowering girls to pursue education.

Emergency Relief

Offer rapid assistance to families during crises, providing food, shelter, and medical supplies.
